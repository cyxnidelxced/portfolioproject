About Me

Hello, I'm Jessica

Hi! I’m Jessica Nguyen, an Interaction Design major based in the Bay Area. I specialize in creating clean, user-friendly websites with a focus on both design and functionality. With experience in web design, I love transforming ideas into digital experiences that are both visually appealing and easy to use.

My passion for web design started as a spark of interest that quickly grew into a career. I’m always excited to learn new tools and techniques to improve my work and stay on top of design trends. When I’m not designing, you can find me at home reading a good novel or listening to some awesome music. Feel free to reach out if you’d like to collaborate or chat about design!
